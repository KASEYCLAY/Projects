Welcome to Bazelat Glamping Retreat

Your serene escape in the heart of central France

Our retreat offers a blend of rustic charm and modern comfort. Nestled in the picturesque commune of Bazelat, our glamping huts provide an immersive experience in nature with all the amenities you need.

Discover Bazelat

Bazelat is a small, charming commune in central France. Known for its historical architecture, friendly locals, and scenic countryside, it offers a perfect getaway from the urban rush. Wander through its quaint streets, visit local markets, and enjoy the slow pace of life in an authentic French setting.
